Getting started

Once you've downloaded AppBox, here's what to do next.

How to use AppBox

Step Description
1. Link your Dropbox account with AppBox.
2. Select and upload the iOS application "OR" select project/workspace to archive and upload the iOS application.
3. Send the link to your testers, clients, friends or even use it yourself.
4. Open the link in the browser on the device and click on install.

AppBox Feature

S.No. Features Supported?
1. Auto App Signing
2. Auto Upload after Archive
3. Keep same link for same app
4. Install previous version with same link
5. Auto E-Mail App Distribution Link
6. Auto Shutdown Mac after Distribution
7. Auto Restart Upload After Network Change or Failure
8. Maximum Upload Size Equal to your Dropbox account storage
9. Uploaded App Expiration Until you delete from your Dropbox
10. Installations Per App Unlimited
10. Jenkins Support In Progress
11. Share builds on local network Comming Soon
12. Dashboard Comming Soon
