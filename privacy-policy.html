Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your data when using AppBox.

Privacy First Approach

AppBox is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data necessary for functionality and never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Overview

This privacy policy ("Policy") applies to the AppBox ("AppBox", "we", "us", "our", getappbox.com, web.getappbox.com, install.getappbox.com, status.getappbox.com, appbox.me) on all platforms (macOS App, Web, Mobile Web, Apps, Emails). By using AppBox, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your Personal Information (as defined below) in accordance with this Policy.

Information We Collect

  • AppBox macOS application doesn't collect any personally identifiable data.
  • AppBox macOS application generates a short URL (appbox.me) through a self-hosted open-source tool YOURURLS, which does not disclose personal information. It stores the following data
    • Public IP
    • Long Install URL
    • Short Install URL
    • Number of requests for a short URL

Third party services

When using AppBox, you must also agree to the privacy policies and terms of service of the third-party services we use.

Dropbox

AppBox uses Dropbox to host your IPA and metadata files. The AppBox macOS application stores your auth token locally in your macOS keychain. We don't store your login information or authentication token on our server.

  • Dropbox Privacy Policy
  • Dropbox Terms of Service

Mailgun

Mailgun is used by AppBox to send emails. All Mailgun requests are processed client-side and no email data is collected by AppBox.

  • Mailgun Privacy Policy
  • Mailgun Terms of Service

Google

AppBox Web installation application uses Google Analytics and Google Adsense

  • Google Policies

Changes to this Policy

We may change this Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our homepage).

Contact Us

If you have questions about privacy or any grievance relating to the processing of information provided by you, you may contact us at developerinsider.co@gmail.com.
